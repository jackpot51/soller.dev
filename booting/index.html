Booting

How modern computers get from the power button to your desktop.

By Jeremy Soller

This blog series explores the "magic" of the boot process of the modern computer. From the moment you press the power button, through the firmware initializing hardware, to the bootloader, kernel, and drivers initializing the hardware yet again (out of spite for the firmware), and finally to the wonderful desktop environments we use for so much today. This will be moderately technical, but I hope to briefly explain most concepts as they show up. Please feel free to report issues or provide changes on the soller.dev GitHub repository.
